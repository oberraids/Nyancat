Nyan Is A Memecoin Launched On BSC. The Cryptocurrency Was Created As A Tribute To The Nyan Cat Internet Meme, Created By Chris Torres, Which Gained Popularity In The Early 2000s.
The Project Aims To Capitalize On The Popularity Of Meme Coins, Such As Shiba Inu And Dogecoin, And Strives To Establish Itself As One Of The Top Meme-Based Cryptocurrencies.
